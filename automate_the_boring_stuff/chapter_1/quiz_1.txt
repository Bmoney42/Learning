5.  This chapter introduced assignment statements, like spam = 10. What is the difference between an expression and a statement?

A:
Expression: evaluates to a value; can appear inside statements.

Statement: performs an action (e.g., assignment) and doesn’t itself produce a value you can use. In the REPL, expressions display their value, but that display isn’t what makes them expressions.
